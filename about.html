About GreenThread

Our Mission

At GreenThread, we believe that fashion can be both stylish and sustainable. Our mission is to create eco-friendly clothing that doesn't compromise on quality or design, while minimizing our impact on the environment.

We're committed to using only sustainable materials, ensuring fair labor practices, and maintaining a carbon-neutral production process. Our goal is to lead the fashion industry towards a more sustainable future, one garment at a time.

Our Values

Sustainability

We use eco-friendly materials and processes to minimize our environmental impact.

Quality

We believe sustainable fashion should be durable and long-lasting.

Innovation

We constantly seek new ways to improve our products and processes.

Transparency

We're open about our practices and supply chain to build trust with our customers.

Our Journey

2015

The Beginning

GreenThread was founded by Sarah and Mike Johnson, two fashion industry veterans who wanted to make a difference. They started with a small collection of organic cotton t-shirts.

2017

Expansion

We opened our first physical store in San Francisco and expanded our product line to include accessories made from recycled materials. Our online presence grew, reaching customers nationwide.

2020

Carbon Neutral

After years of hard work, we achieved our goal of becoming a carbon-neutral company. We implemented innovative recycling programs and partnered with environmental organizations to offset our emissions.

2023

Global Reach

GreenThread products are now available in over 20 countries. We've collaborated with international designers and expanded our range to include sustainable formal wear and activewear.

Our Materials

We carefully select our materials to ensure they meet our high standards for sustainability and quality:

Organic Cotton

Grown without harmful pesticides or synthetic fertilizers.

Recycled Polyester

Made from post-consumer plastic bottles, reducing waste.

Tencel

A sustainable fabric made from wood pulp in a closed-loop process.

Hemp

A durable, naturally pest-resistant crop that requires minimal water.

Our Commitment to Fair Labor

We believe in ethical production and fair treatment of workers. All our partner factories are regularly audited to ensure they meet our strict standards for worker safety, fair wages, and good working conditions. We're proud members of the Fair Wear Foundation and are committed to continual improvement in our supply chain.
